The purpose of this site is to offer a comteplative discovery of the universe at first, while defining clearly the important terms.



















I am a student in my 3rd year of engineering school, I would like to specialize in the environment next year, and in the long term I would ideally like to work in the field of astrophysics.

I have always been passionate about astronomy, I have always inquired and learned on my own, I was always lulled by the stories of Hubert Reeves, Stephen Hawking, Etienne Klein and many other so I am really happy to be able to express it in an artistic way.
I decided to go to Bangor during my international semester, for the living environment, the green and refreshing landscapes, unless it's to finally be able to do like Harry Potter in the libraries of course!
In my opinion, the engineering curriculum lacks a creative side, so being able to choose your theme freely is a very good educational choice for me.
The purpose of this site is purely contemplative, I unfortunately can't deal with all the fascinating subjects related to astrophysics simply because of lack of time, but I will continue to improve my site as my knowledge grows.
